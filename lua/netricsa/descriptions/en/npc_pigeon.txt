PIGEON


CLASS:      Urban Avian
METABOLISM: Bird
PERCEPTION: Moderate (basic awareness, flock coordination)
SIZE:       ~1-1.5 ft
ENDURANCE:  Low
HOSTILITY:  Low
WEAPONS:    Beak, claws
REWARD:     2 FC
THREAT:     Very Low

DESCRIPTION:

Pigeons are common in urban areas, coexisting with humans and other wildlife. They are passive, often seen in flocks on rooftops, streets, and squares. While harmless, they can react to sudden disturbances, flying away and creating visual cues.
