AK-47


WEAPON:      AK-47 Assault Rifle
AMMO:        7.62x39mm rounds
ROF:         ~600 rounds/min

DESCRIPTION:

Iconic automatic rifle known for reliability and durability. Fires powerful 7.62mm rounds with moderate recoil. Effective at medium range and capable of sustained fire for suppression or direct engagements.

NOTES:

- Highly durable and simple to operate.
- Moderate recoil; controllable in short bursts.
- Effective against unarmored and lightly armored targets.
- Magazine capacity requires periodic reloads during prolonged combat.
