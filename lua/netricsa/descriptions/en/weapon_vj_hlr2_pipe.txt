METAL PIPE


WEAPON:      Metal Pipe
ROF:         ~60 swings/min

DESCRIPTION:

Improvised melee weapon used by City 17 refugees and civilians. Simple but sturdy, the metal pipe delivers blunt-force damage at close range. Primarily a last-resort weapon for self-defense against Combine forces or headcrabs.

NOTES:

- Unlimited use; no ammo required.
- Effective in close quarters only.
- Deals moderate blunt damage; can stagger weak enemies.
- Functions as a practical tool in survival situations.
