CIRCULAR SAW


WEAPON: Circular Saw
AMMO:   None 
ROF:    Continuous while holding trigger 

DESCRIPTION:

A melee tool upgraded for combat. Circular saw with a chrome blade and a yellow body featuring a small screen and exhaust pipes. The saw delivers high damage in close range, especially against weak enemies and destructible props.

NOTES:

- Max damage per slice: 50. 
- Close range only; not effective against long-range shooters. 
- Excellent for clearing crowds of weaker foes like Lizards, Spiky Bulldogs, Kleer Skeletons without using ranged ammo. 
- Useful for breaking crates and destructible world objects with no ammo cost.
