GMOD CAMERA


WEAPON: Camera Tool

DESCRIPTION:

Utility tool used to capture images and record scenes in-game. Allows the take screenshots from any angle, adjust zoom, and control focus. Primarily intended for creative content and documentation rather than combat.

NOTES:

- Infinite use; no ammo required.
- Adjustable field of view and zoom.
- Can be combined with other tools for cinematic shots.
- Non-lethal; purely creative utility.
