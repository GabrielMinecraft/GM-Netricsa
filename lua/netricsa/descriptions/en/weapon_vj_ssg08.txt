SSG-08 SCOUT SNIPER RIFLE


WEAPON: SSG-08 Sniper Rifle
AMMO: 7.62x51mm rounds
ROF: Bolt-action, ~25 rds/min

DESCRIPTION:

Lightweight, bolt-action sniper rifle designed for fast deployment and mobility. Ideal for marksmen who need precision without the bulk of heavier sniper rifles. Fires high-velocity rounds capable of neutralizing enemies at medium to long ranges with a single well-placed shot.

NOTES:

- Extremely light and easy to maneuver compared to heavy sniper rifles.
- Moderate recoil; allows for quick follow-up shots.
- Equipped with standard scope for long-range targeting.
- Limited ammo requires careful shot placement.
- Favored for mobility and rapid repositioning in combat scenarios.
