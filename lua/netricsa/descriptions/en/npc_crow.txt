CROW


CLASS:      Wild Avian
METABOLISM: Bird
PERCEPTION: High (keen eyesight, alert to threats)
SIZE:       ~1.5 ft
ENDURANCE:  Low
HOSTILITY:  Low (defensive if threatened)
WEAPONS:    Beak, claws
REWARD:     1 FC
THREAT:     Very Low

DESCRIPTION:

Crows are intelligent, wary birds often found in urban and rural areas. They gather in flocks and can mob potential threats, using coordinated calls to alert others. While not aggressive by default, their presence can signal danger or environmental change.

TIPS:

- Flocks may indicate nearby enemies or hazards.
