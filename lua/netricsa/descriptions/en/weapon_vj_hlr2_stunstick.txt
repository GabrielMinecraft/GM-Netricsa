COMBINE STUN STICK


WEAPON:      Combine Stun Stick
AMMO:        None (melee, electric)
ROF:         ~90 strikes/min

DESCRIPTION:

Electrified melee weapon used by Combine soldiers for close-quarters combat. Delivers powerful electric shocks capable of incapacitating most organic targets instantly. Lightweight and effective for crowd control or as a last-resort weapon.

NOTES:

- High melee damage with stunning effect.
- Unlimited use, no ammo required.
- Effective only at close range.
- Can be combined with other attacks for maximum crowd control.
