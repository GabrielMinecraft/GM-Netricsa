TOOL GUN


WEAPON:      Tool Gun

DESCRIPTION:

Multi-functional utility device for manipulating objects and game mechanics. Allows the weld, freeze, constrain, and adjust properties of props and entities. Essential for building, customizing, and experimenting in the  environment.

NOTES:

- Infinite use; no ammo required.
- Supports dozens of tools for construction, physics, and scripting.
- Can interact with both props and creatures depending on the selected tool.
- Non-lethal; purely for creative and experimental purposes.
