RPG


WEAPON:      RPG (Rocket-Propelled Grenade Launcher)
AMMO:        High-explosive rockets
ROF:         ~4 rockets/min

DESCRIPTION:

Heavy anti-armor weapon system with laser-guided targeting. Delivers extreme destructive power, capable of destroying vehicles, turrets, and large hostile organisms.

NOTES:

- Enormous blast radius, lethal to groups.
- Laser guidance ensures precise hits.
- Dangerous to fire in confined spaces.
