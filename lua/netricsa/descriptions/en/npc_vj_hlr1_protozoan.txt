THE PROTOZOAN FROM XEN


CLASS:      Xen, Aerial Microbe
METABOLISM: gaseous filter-feeder
PERCEPTION: passive
SIZE:       3-4 ft diameter
ENDURANCE:  indefinite (non-living)
HOSTILITY:  none
WEAPONS:    none
REWARD:     15 FC
THREAT:     none

DESCRIPTION:

The Protozoan is a passive, non-hostile organism native to Xen. It appears as a translucent, bluish orb with a smaller reddish sphere inside, orbiting gently above the ground. The Protozoan serves no combat function and poses no threat. 

TIPS:

- No action is required; the Protozoan does not engage or pose any danger.
