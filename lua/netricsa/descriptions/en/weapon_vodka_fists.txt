POWER FISTS OF VODKA


WEAPON:      Spirit-Infused Fists
AMMO:        None (melee)
ROF:         ~75 strikes/min

DESCRIPTION:

Fists charged by Vodka. Blows land with supernatural force, and each strike carries the raw burn of energy coursing through the drinker's body.

NOTES:

- Strong melee with reliable force.
- Alcohol effect remains active, slightly affecting movement.
- Effective in sudden close-quarters encounters.
