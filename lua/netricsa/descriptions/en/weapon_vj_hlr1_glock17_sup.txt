9MM SILENCED PISTOL

Standard 9mm semi-automatic pistol fitted with a suppressor. Issued to Black Ops female assassins for stealth operations within Black Mesa. Delivers accurate, quiet shots, allowing operatives to eliminate targets without alerting nearby enemies.
