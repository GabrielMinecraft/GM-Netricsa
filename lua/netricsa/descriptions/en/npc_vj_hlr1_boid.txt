BOID FROM XEN


CLASS:      Alien, Xen
METABOLISM: unknown
PERCEPTION: social flyer
SIZE:       2.5 ft wingspan
ENDURANCE:  low
HOSTILITY:  none
WEAPONS:    none
REWARD:     50 FC
THREAT:     none

DESCRIPTION:

Boids are even friendly cousins of Aflocks, flying in tight flocks and creating mesmerizing aerial patterns. They are completely passive and harmless, mostly curious about their environment. When seen together, their coordinated movements look like a living alien birds dance.

TIPS:

- Do not attack; they will not fight back.
- Watch their flocks for a calming alien spectacle.
