PHEROPOD


WEAPON:      PHEROPOD
ROF:         ~1 throw/sec

DESCRIPTION:

Organic weapon used to control antlions. Upon throwing, the larvae release pheromones that attract nearby antlions to attack enemies. Serves as both a tactical distraction and offensive support tool.

NOTES:

- Effective for crowd control and redirecting enemy attention.
- Infinite supply as long as larvae are available in inventory.
- Requires careful timing and placement for maximum effect.
- Best used in areas where antlions are present.
