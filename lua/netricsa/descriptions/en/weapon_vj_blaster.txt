E-5 BLASTER RIFLE


WEAPON:      E-5 Blaster Rifle
AMMO:        Energy cells
ROF:         ~600 shots/min

DESCRIPTION:

Standard-issue blaster rifle for elite units. Fires rapid energy bolts capable of piercing light armor and causing significant damage at medium range. Reliable, lightweight, and effective in both suppression and precision fire roles.

NOTES:

- High rate of fire with moderate recoil.
- Effective against infantry and lightly armored targets.
- Energy cells must be monitored to avoid running out mid-combat.
