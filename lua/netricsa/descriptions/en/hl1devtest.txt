BLACK MESA DEVTEST, STRANGE


Instead of routine experiments and idle chatter, the directly into chaos. Almost immediately, the Black Mesa facility comes under heavy bombardment, with explosions shaking the corridors, ceiling tiles crashing down, and emergency sirens blaring.
