RAT FROM EARTH


CLASS:      Hazardous Environment Rodent
METABOLISM: Mammalian
PERCEPTION: Visual + smell
SIZE:       ~1 ft (30 cm)
ENDURANCE:  Low
HOSTILITY:  Low
WEAPONS:    none
REWARD:     5 FC
THREAT:     Negligible

DESCRIPTION:

Standard Black Mesa rats found in facility corridors and maintenance areas. Mostly passive and scurry away from humans, causing minor nuisance.

TIPS:

- Harmless - ignore unless needed for objectives.
- Can be used to distract enemies in tight spaces.
